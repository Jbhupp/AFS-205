Welcome to my custom apparel page!

On this page I will share a small portions of the designs that we have created and printed or embroidered on apparel or hats.

Please feel free to leave a comment or suggest a new design!

A few short years back my sister, mom and myself deciding to start a little side business of creating designs for apparel or anything you need it on. We have always had our own businesses and decided to try our hand at a new one. It didn't take long before I fell in love with creating the designs, printing them on garments and seeing the final result. We try our best to recreate your design you have or we create one from start to finish for you!
All designs on this page are property of Jenni Hupp. All designs were created for customers who sent us what they had in mind and we put created it. If you would like to obtain a design please let me know.
